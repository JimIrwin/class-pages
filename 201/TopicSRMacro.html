Topic 7: SR Macro   -- preliminary web page --

Short-run Macro -- performance of the economy from month to month, quarter to quarter, or year to year.  Focussed on gdp-growth and unemployment.

The central concern of short-run macro is  "Business Cycles:" recurrent fluctuations of GDP-growth. Also concerned with the related fluctuations in  U (the unemployment rate).  

Fluctuations relative to 'normal' or 'usual'  ...   SR macro:  Good times /Normal  /Bad times  (define w.r.t. LR economic growth).

Business Cycles in theory are regular fluctuations in gdp-growth (with associated fluctations unemployment).
Business Cycles in reality are not regular and not predictable: graphs of the key SR macro indicators :      U & GDP-growth    Make your own graphs

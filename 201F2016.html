Reading assignment #1  (for week 1 and test 1). Saylor: Preface, Chapter 1, Appendix A; OpenStax, Chapter 1 and The Use of Mathematics in Principles of Economics. Look over these materials for orientation, then for test 1, focus on the following: economics, scarcity, choice, cost, maximization, choices at the margin, micro vs macro, scientific method, models (theory), ceterus paribus, false cause, normative vs positive.

The appendix materials on graphing should be review; if you have questions or concerns about the appendix material, raise them in class or on Piazza.

Video assignment #1 for week 1 and test 1 (in week 3 or 4): watch the orientation video from the AEA and be prepared to answer simple questions to demonstrate that you paid attention (background info).

Reading Assignment # 3 supporting Topic 4 (and covered in OnLine Test 2, week of 4/13-17). UMN Principles of MacroChapter 6 [ alternate link], Sections 6.1 and 6.2. Look over sections 6.1 & 6.2 (skipping the part on GNP), then for online testing focus on the following: the four major spending components of GDP (these are important: be able to identify and describe each), final goods and value added, the value-added tax, the components of GDI (be able to identify and describe the largest component; be able to recognize the others). I encourage you to skip the last section, "Tracing Income from the Economy to Households." Optionally, Section 1 of Chapter 6 of the OpenStax textbook also covers most of the material for this assignment, but it there is a lot of other material as well, and it is quite long. So maybe have a look, in case it helps; but don't hesitate to ignore it.

Territorial Expansion and Settlement

1783 the area of the USA is 889 Ksm (thousand square-miles). At the end of revolutionary war, the USA is comprised of the 13 former colonies, plus almost all land south of the Great Lakes and east of the Mississippi.

1803 827 Ksm the Louisiana purchase almost doubles the country's land-mass.

1819 72 Ksm added by the acquisition of Florida, from Spain.

1845 390 Ksm added bythe Annexation of Texas (and most of New Mexico). Much interesting history here, such as The US-Mexican War, Forgotten Foes (which connects to our discussions of Amerindian populations and the N-S reversal.

1848 529 Ksm added by the Mexican Cession; war on Mexico yields the lands of Calif, Nev, Utah, N-Ariz N-NewM, adding more than 1/2 the original area). For historians, Manifest Injustice"; this Masters' thesis by a Canadian military officer offers an interesting normative perspective.

1846 Oregon Territory286 Ksm (US gets Wash Oreg Idah; Britain keeps what is now British Columbia, Canada)

1867 586 Ksm

Also: Hawaii, 10.9 Ksm in 1898; Puerto Rico, 5.3 Ksm in 1899).
